How to Use the Coffee Maker

  1. Plug in and fill: Plug the machine in and fill the water tank with fresh water
  2. Place mug: Put a mug or cup on the removable drip tray. The tray is adjustable for different cup sizes.
  3. Open brew head: Lift the top of the machine to open the brew head.
  4. Insert T-Disc: Take a T-Disc (TASSIMO pod) and place it in the brew head with the barcode facing down. The disc should be properly seated.
  5. Close brew head: Close the top of the machine until you hear it click into place.
  6. Start the drink: Press the start button. The machine will read the barcode and begin brewing automatically.
  7. Wait for completion: Wait until the brewing is finished and the light stops flashing to safely remove your mug.
  8. Remove and enjoy: Remove your mug and add any desired additions like milk or sugar.
  9. For multi-disc drinks: For drinks like a cappuccino, use two T-Discs in sequence (e.g., the milk pod first, then the coffee pod).
